Title: How to play video on Sugar
URL: https://wiki.sugarlabs.org/go/How_to_play_video_on_Sugar
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
video playback on Sugar
go to root "su" in the terminal
type "yum update"
accept changes and let yum install updates (good idea?!)
try "yum install vlc"
or
try
yum install jukebox
or
try
yum install mplayer
Your mileage may vary.
.ogg videos just play from the journal, one click and they play.
