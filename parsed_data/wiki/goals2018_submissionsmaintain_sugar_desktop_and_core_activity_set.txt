Title: Goals/2018 Submissions/Maintain Sugar desktop and core activity set
URL: https://wiki.sugarlabs.org/go/Goals/2018_Submissions/Maintain_Sugar_desktop_and_core_activity_set
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Proposal
Maintain in working order the Sugar desktop and a core set of Sugar activities against the two popular and always changing Linux distributions; Fedora and Debian.
Comments
In the past year, apart from me (James Cameron), I've found almost nobody interested enough in this goal to exert continuous effort; though there are always people willing to provide speculation.
