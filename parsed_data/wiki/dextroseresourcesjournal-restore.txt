JOURNAL RESTORE
(Documented on)
Hardware    :    Sugar XO-1.5
Build       :    373pyg Paraguay
Sugar       :    0.88.1
Firmware    :    Q3A50
NOTE:   This document has been prepared to provide the reader an idea of the working and
use of the Journal Restore feature added in the recent builds of Sugar Dextrose.
To use the Journal Restore feature You would require:
1) A Sugar XO-1 or XO-1.5 or Sugar emulator running the latest build of Sugar Dextrose.
2) A USB-2.0 flash drive (preferably atleast 1gb in size)
To Begin
Start the system running the latest build of Sugar Dextrose .
Plugin a usb-2.0 flash drive device into the usb port .
Screenshot of Journal before Restore
Open the journal and right click on the usb drive symbol in the journal.
Screenshot of the right click action on the usb drive icon in journal
Now to Restore the Journal contents click on the Restore journal option
Screenshot  of the pre-Restore Stage of the Restore Journal Feature GUI.
Now click on the Start button to Restore the journal contents from the usb flashdrive.
Screenshot of the post-Restore Stage of the Restore Journal Feature GUI
Now click on the restart button at the completion of the Restore process . After restart you can see the  journal contents has been restored back to that of the time of the backup.
Screenshot of the journal after the restore process
