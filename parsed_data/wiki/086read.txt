What is new for users
The read activity now has support for
Epub
files. For downloading Epub content,
http://www.feedbooks.com
and
http://www.epubbooks.com/
are recommended place to start looking. Furthermore this release will let you associate (add and edit) notes with your bookmarks, and has a slightly more useful fullscreen view (you can now get a rough idea of how much battery is left in your laptop/netbook without invoking the frame).
Read
displaying an Epub book, a bookmark with text notes is also being displaying for the page.
