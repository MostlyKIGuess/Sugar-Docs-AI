This page is about
Adobe Flash
, a proprietary player for Flash media.  For the free software player that comes with Sugar, see Gnash (need sugarlabs link).
Overview
Adobe Flash
10 can be installed for the Browse Activity, the Opera activity and the Surf Activity.  This installation must be repeated after any update.
Instructions for installing Adobe Flash can be found on the OLPC wiki at
Adobe Flash
