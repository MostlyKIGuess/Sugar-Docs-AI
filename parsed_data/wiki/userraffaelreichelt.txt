Raffael Reichelt is an educational activist living in Berlin, Germany .
Last year he decided to quit his IT management job in order to dedicate his talents to something more persistent than running large scale ERP and DWH systems. Actually he is setting up
Learning Studios
for children to promote Sugar/
Sugar on a Stick
in Berlin and Germany.
This year he organized  "Sugar Labs presence" at
LinuxTag 2011
in Berlin.
With his project proposal for learning studios, he was awarded the first prize at
"Berlins Zukunft ist offen"
project and idea contest by the senate of Berlin.
