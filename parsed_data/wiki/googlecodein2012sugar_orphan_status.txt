Background
Sugar Activity developer come and go, but the code they write is with us forever. And yet since Sugar changes, activities inevitably need maintenance and updating.
Task
Identify which Sugar Activities have been orphaned and develop and document a mechanism in the Sugar Labs wiki for assigning maintainers to these orphaned activities.

The Sugar Activity Team
