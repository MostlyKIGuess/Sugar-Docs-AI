Meetings
The Infrastructure Team holds weekly meetings on Tuesdays at 21:00 UTC (16:00 EST, 22:00 CET) on irc.freenode.net (channel: #sugar-meeting). Please add your topics to the agenda below and join in with your ideas.

Adding topics
During the week, you can add topics that you want to discuss during the meeting to the above list. They will be collected and announced in the channel when the meeting opens.
Participating
The Infrastructure Team meeting is mainly targeted at Sugar Labs system administrators, but everyone interested is welcome to join. Its primary purpose is to attempt to make our work more transparent and allow the community to participate.
