Meetings
meetings should be announced in advance on the sugar and iaep mailing lists
please make sure to have your meeting listed in the
calendar
as well
the reminder should state in the header the time (in UTC), the date, and the place, and whether this is a Reminder or the Minutes of the meeting
example: Sugar-meeting REMINDER (Thursday November 13 2008 - 14.00 (UTC)) --- irc.freenode.net, #sugar-meeting
This template in the body of the note will be processed into a sidebar calendar link for Gmail users:
What: Sugar meeting template (use the word meeting someplace)
When: 19 February 2009,  1:00 pm EST    (n.b., time zones are not interpreted for you, so be careful)
Where:
irc://irc.freenode.net#sugar-meeting
