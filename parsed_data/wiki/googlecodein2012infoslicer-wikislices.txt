Background
Infoslicer is a Sugar activity that lets teachers create custom articles by grabbing content from the Wikipedia and repackaging it for distribution in the classroom. Wikislices is a Sugar activity that provides a local copy of a subset (slice) of the Wikipedia. Infoslicer works with on-line resources, but is not able to integrate material from the off-line Wikislice. See
