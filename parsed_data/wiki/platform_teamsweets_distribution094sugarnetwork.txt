Sugar Network integration
By default,
Sugar Network
integration is disabled in the Sugar Shell. To enable it, go to the
Plugins
component in the Control Panel, switch-on the integration checkbox, and restart Sugar. After restarting, the Sugar Network client will be available from the Activities tray and the
F8
shortcut key.
