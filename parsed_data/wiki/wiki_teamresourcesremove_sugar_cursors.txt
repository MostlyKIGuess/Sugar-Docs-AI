Title: Wiki Team/Resources/Remove Sugar Cursors
URL: https://wiki.sugarlabs.org/go/Wiki_Team/Resources/Remove_Sugar_Cursors
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
If you prefer not to experience the Sugar cursors, take these steps to override them for your browser:
Visit the
Appearance tab
of your wiki preferences.
Copy the text in gray below into the edit window for the 'Custom CSS' page for your selected skin.
Save the page.

  border-bottom: 1px dashed Grey;      
  cursor: help;      
}
