Title: GoogleCodeIn2012/Multiple homeview
URL: https://wiki.sugarlabs.org/go/GoogleCodeIn2012/Multiple_homeview
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Background
Sugar displays favorite activities on the home view. But it would be nice to have different collections of activities for school, home, play, etc.
Task
Add an option to have different collections of activities on the Home View for formal (classroom) and informal (home) use.
