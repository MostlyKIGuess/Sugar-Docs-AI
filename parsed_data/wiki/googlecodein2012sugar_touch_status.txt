Title: GoogleCodeIn2012/Sugar touch status
URL: https://wiki.sugarlabs.org/go/GoogleCodeIn2012/Sugar_touch_status
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Background
Sugar now support touch and an on-screen keyboard. But most Sugar activities do not take advantage of these features and are therefore not well suited for (or optimized for) tablet use.
Task
Identify which Sugar Activities could be enhanced by touch features and which activities could make use of on-screen keyboard support.
Contact
The Sugar Activity Team
