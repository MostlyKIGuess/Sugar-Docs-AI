Introduction
HorseGame is a simple game where you play with a small horse.
Click or press 'a' to drop an apple.  Press 'c' to drop a carrot and 'h' to drop a hay.
The horse will run over and eat the food.
Future Enhancements
Allow two or more laptops to play together (each child has her own horse).
Allow choice of colours for the horse.
Convert images to svg format.
Development
