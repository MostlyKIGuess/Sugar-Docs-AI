JT Mengel is a student at Rochester Institute of Technology in upstate New York pursuing his undergraduate degree from the Golisano Center of Computer and Information Sciences.  He is particularly interested in the techniques and progress of modern animation and graphical, educational and medical applications of emerging technology.
Robotics, programming, traditional studio art, and learning - JT is a contributer to the Open Source community as a graphical program and generator of art assets.  He is also known for referring to himself in the third person for extended periods.
