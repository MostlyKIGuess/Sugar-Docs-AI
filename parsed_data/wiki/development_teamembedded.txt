There are now plans to port  Sugar into some embedded environments.
Projects
BeagleBoard
Welcome to the Sugar on the BeagleBoard project.
Background
The initial port will be getting Sugar[1] to run on the Beagleboard[2]
using the Open Embedded[3] toolkit.
For a collaboration point, there is a sugar on beagle wiki[4].
For Sugar related bug, Sugar Labs uses [5]. Open Embedded
uses bugzilla[6].
If you are interested come introduce yourself on the beagleboard[7]
mailing list.
