Title: Wiki Team/Guide/Watchlist
URL: https://wiki.sugarlabs.org/go/Wiki_Team/Guide/Watchlist
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
You can add any page to your
watchlist
, in which case you will receive an e-mail notice when someone else makes a change to that page.
To
watch
an article simply click on the
watch
tab at the top of the page.   Click that same tab on a watched page to
unwatch
it.
There is also a checkbox under the "Summary:" line on the article editing page which allows you to watch a page.
Follow the
my watchlist
link in the upper right corner of any page to view and manage your watch list.
See the
watchlist
tab in
my preferences
to control your watchlist.
