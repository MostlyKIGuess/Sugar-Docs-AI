This page is an incomplete attempt to classify some Sugar videos (not updated since December 2011).
The aim had been to flag up videos which should be linked to
Getting Started/Explore
, should be viewable in Sugar, and should directly address those starting to get to grips with Sugar. A few videos are linked from that page.
Personal considerations for using video at "Getting Started" level.
These videos were once the core of the Getting Started page.
Videos embedded in a wiki page may hamper loading the page in Sugar and low resource machines.
Learners may be impatient, video should grab attention and directly address those
starting
to get to grips with 
Sugar.
There may be only one computer available. Ideally materials should be viewable in Sugar.
There are some examples of high quality video presentation at
http://www.waveplace.org/courseware/basic-etoys/
and
One Laptop per Child Australia XO Training Course
. Guests may view the course.
