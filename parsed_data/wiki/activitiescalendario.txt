About Calendario
Calendario is a Sugar Activity for manage tasks in a calendar interface. This application helps the development of the organizational skill on the children.
Features
Pending tasks.
Relevant content like daily ephemeris and phrase of the week.
Categories for activities.
Prioritize (mark important) activities.
State per activities (In progress or completed).
Allow to import ephemeris and phrase.
