Title: Sugar Labs/Governance/Membership
URL: https://wiki.sugarlabs.org/go/Sugar_Labs/Governance/Membership
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Details
The Sugar Labs membership elects the
Oversight Board
each August, for terms beginning in September.
Members
can nominate themselves to run in the election. The overall election process is overseen by the
Membership and Elections Committee
, which can be reached at elections at sugarlabs.org.
