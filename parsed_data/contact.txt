Join us at Sugar-devel mailing list
http://lists.sugarlabs.org/listinfo/sugar-devel.
We have a Matrix channel where real time discussions mostly happen. 
Some important channels are:
* #sugar:matrix.org: General Sugar Labs discussion
* #sugar-systems:matrix.org: Questions and discussion about Sugar Labs Infrastructure.
* #music-blocks:matrix.org: Share your projects and asks questions about Music Blocks (musicblocks.sugarlabs.org)
* #musicblocksdev:matrix.org: Development for Music Blocks visual programming language.
For Sugarizer specific discussions, join the Discord server.
